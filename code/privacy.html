Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the official website of Team Eric Wu, operating under Ray White Elite. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe, transparent, and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

2. Information We Collect

In the course of providing premium real estate services in East Auckland, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Property Information: Details regarding your property when you request a free appraisal, including address, property features, and your timeline for selling.
  • Technical Data: Information such as your IP address, browser type, and interactions with our website (via cookies) to help us improve our digital services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We collect your personal information strictly for the purpose of conducting our real estate business. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Provide you with accurate property appraisals and market insights.
  • Communicate with you regarding properties for sale, auction updates, or relevant real estate news.
  • Facilitate the buying or selling process, including preparing legal documents and marketing materials.
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations, including the Anti-Money Laundering and Countering Financing of Terrorism (AML/CFT) Act 2009.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Ray White Network: With the wider Ray White corporate network for administrative, compliance, or marketing purposes as part of our franchise agreement.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business (such as marketing agencies or IT support), or servicing you, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law to disclose information to government or regulatory authorities.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure your personal information is protected from loss, unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure. Your data is stored securely on encrypted digital platforms and internal Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems utilized by Team Eric Wu and Ray White.

6. Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to ask for it to be corrected if you believe it is inaccurate. If you wish to be removed from our mailing lists or marketing databases, you can opt-out at any time by contacting us.
